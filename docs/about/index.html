About

This site is currently in testing. Some events may be missing, duplicated, or incorrect whilst I'm getting things set up.

cov.kids is a community events listing for Coventry and the surrounding area. It pulls together gigs, markets, comedy nights, theatre, sport, film screenings, and more from a range of local sources, all in one place.

How it works

Events are pulled in from Reddit (/r/coventry), Eventbrite, venue websites, email newsletters, and CVFolk, an amazing resource if you're into folk music in the area. A bit of AI and other magic helps extract the details (name, date, venue, category) from all that. Everything is then reviewed before it appears on the site.

Inspiration

Heavily inspired by u/HadjiChippoSafri on /r/coventry, who has been posting weekly "What's On" threads for the community for years.

Area filters

By default the site shows events in Coventry itself. Use the + Warwickshire and + Birmingham pills to broaden the search to nearby towns (Leamington, Warwick, Bedworth, Kenilworth, Nuneaton, Rugby, Stratford...) or to Birmingham gigs. Birmingham listings are pretty sparse for now but I'll get there.

Something missing?

Know of an event or a local listings source I should be picking up? Get in touch and I'll add it.

Got a flyer?

Spotted something on a poster or received a flyer? Forward it to ck@kev.cc and I'll try to get it on.

Run a venue or event?

If you have a mailing list, add ck@kev.cc and your events should start appearing automatically. No mailing list? Just email the details directly to ck@kev.cc and I'll get them on.

I can also pull events directly from a Facebook page. Just let me know your Facebook address and I'll set it up.
